About Me

My journey to Addis Ababa University's software engineering program wasn't a straightforward path; it was forged through dedication and resilience. From navigating the challenges of my elementary and high school years to achieving my academic goals, I've come to appreciate the importance of persistence, which I apply to each line of code I create.

Background

From a young age, I've been driven by a thirst for knowledge and a fascination with how things work. This innate curiosity, coupled with a lifelong engagement with technology, naturally led me to pursue a career in software engineering. The constant evolution and innovative spirit of the industry perfectly align with my desire to continuously learn and contribute to something meaningful.

Skills

My skillset encompasses a strong foundation in programming with Java and Python, complemented by proficiency in data structures and algorithms (DSA). I'm also experienced in database management using MySQL, possess a working knowledge of networking fundamentals, and am familiar with front-end development using HTML and CSS. I'm continually seeking opportunities to expand my expertise and tackle new challenges.

My Passion

My professional aspiration is to become a top-tier full-stack developer, constantly pushing my technical boundaries and delivering high-quality work. Beyond my individual goals, I'm passionate about making a positive impact on my community, specifically by supporting and empowering women pursuing careers in the technology industry.
